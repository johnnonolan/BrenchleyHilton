This self-contained beamed farm workers cottage is set in the country garden of a large Kentish farmhouse. The charming accommodation comprises a twin bedded room, a bathroom, a small sitting room with TV, and a kitchen / breakfast room. Full English or continental breakfast. Private parking.

The cottage is within easy walking distance of local pubs and Brenchley village. It provides an ideal base for touring West Kent and East Sussex. The historic town of Royal Tunbridge Wells is 6 miles away and London is easily accessible via the frequent train service from Paddock Wood, 3 miles away.
